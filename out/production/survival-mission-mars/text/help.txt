Commands can be single or dual word commands.

Common single word commands:
'QUIT'  --  quits the game
'HELP'  --  brings up this help document

Common dual word commands, start with action words:  'GO', 'GET', 'DROP', 'LOOK', 'USE'
...and follow with nouns: 'INVENTORY', 'ROOM', 'OXYGEN', 'CHALLENGE', or an item in your inventory, such as 'KEY'
